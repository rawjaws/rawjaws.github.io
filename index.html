Our Story

  • 2008-2009

    Love at First Sight!

    Mike and Caellie first met shortly after Caellie started working at Price Chopper. We used to try to take our breaks together and would chat any time we could!

  • February 2009

    The First Date

    Mike asked Caellie out on a date, with not much to choose from in Cobleskill and it was the day the whole town lost power, we went to Perone’s for some ice cream in February! Ice cream is for all seasons to Caellie!

  • August 2014

    Moving in Together!

    Mike and Caellie decided to take the leap and move out to Latham into their own place. It was a great adventure for both of them!

  • July 2015

    The Proposal!!

    Mike had finally made the big decision to ask Caellie to marry him! He properly asked her father for her hand and waited until the whole family was on vacation. He got my parents in on it, set it all up to play on of Caellie’s favorites game, Cards Against Humanity. Mike had Caellie go first and draw the Haiku card. His cards, “Caellie, Will you, Marry Me?” She thought he was joking, didn’t think it was to funny! And obviously, she said YES!

  • July 2017

    We’re Getting Married!!

    Mike and Caellie will be getting married on July 7th, 2017 with their families and friends to witness and celebrate our love!
